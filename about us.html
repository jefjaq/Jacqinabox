Jacq in a box was started as a business in 2014 selling giftware we have progressed to selling clothes, jewellery, handbags, balloons. In 2015 the florist next to us was put up for sale we bought it and now include flowers, Jacqueline who owns and runs the business is a florist the business was formerly called Poppies Plants and Flowers. We have incorperated it into Jacq in a Box. We invite you to come along and have a look at the giftware and the flowers. We think you will be delighted at the variety and quality of the products that we sell.

 We also welcome phone orders for Flowers. you can find them under Products in the menu bar.Under the Delivery Area icon you will find the postcodes that we deliver flowers to, it is usually within 30 kilometers of the business.

We can deliver interstate and globally through PETALS we have a page exclusively for arrangements through Petals.
